Fatal Traffic Accidents Between 2014 And 2019

All fatal traffic accidents in Victoria between the years 2014 and 2019 were recorded. Information regarding accident location, individuals involved and details about how the accident occurred were noted and provided by VicRoads. A total of 1358 fatal accidents occured during this time period.

It should be noted that the data provided for 2019 is incompleteand does not cover the entire year of 2019.

Overview of Fatal Traffic Accidents

The following area chart provides an overview of the number of accidents from January 2014 to March 2019. This visualisation demonstrates the shape distribution of fatal traffic accidents. The sharp decline in number from January 2019 onwards is due to the fact that the dataset does not contain information for the entire year of 2019, however the sharp incline to peak at the start of 2019 suggests the possibility that numbers may continue to increase.

Average Ages of Fatal Traffic Accidents by Gender

Age is an attribute often observed when determining the cause of fatal traffic accients. The following bar chart inspects the variation in the average ages of individuals involved in fatal traffic accidents during the time period of January 2014 to March 2019. It can be observed that the range in average ages is much larger for females than males and females in fatal traffic accidents are older on average when compared to males in fatal traffic accidents.
